Stacking up

We were surprised that our personal favorite the MacBook Pro was outscored so badly by HP, Dell, and Toshiba. Part of this is due to personal preference, we prefer MacOS and UNIX instead of Windows. Another fault comes from our assumptions. The ratings online that were such a hefty component of our rankings were done by the average user rather than a programmer and so have different criteria for satisfaction. Also the Mac’s price was a hindrance, part of this stems from the fact that is has such a good display, which adds $200 to the price. These are our personal opinions and we hope that you choose the perfect companion for you that will support you in these coming years. But even if you make a poor choice at least this companion is not eternal.
