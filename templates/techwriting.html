Welcome to the Computer Science major! We hope you enjoy these next four years with a stellar laptop at your side. Let’s be honest, you’re super busy as you transition to this new phase of your life and may not have time to research laptops. Never fear! We have developed a set of criteria to help you choose the laptop that is right for you and ranked seven popular models as examples.

We realize we can't rank every laptop out there so we picked the best selling laptops listed on Amazon (link to page) excluding tablets, chromebooks, and non-Intel CPUs. We chose to gather all our data from Amazon.com because it is the largest online laptop retailer. It is also unaffiliated with specific laptop brands. Therefore, we can assume the information and user ratings are unbiased.

Below you will find explanations of what we measured, how we did it, what we found, and what we thought.

How we did it.

In order to calculate the score for each laptop, we first gave each of the criteria a certain weight, all of which added up to 100. We then developed a way to calculate a score for each criterion based on the specifications of the laptop, which we then scaled to the correct weight. We also needed to determine the highest reasonable limit for each criteria in order to scale each one correctly. To do this, we needed to make some assumptions about the highest reasonable limit for each criteria. What we thought.

We were surprised that our personal favorite, the MacBook Pro, was outscored so badly by HP, Dell, and Toshiba. Part of this is due to personal preference, we prefer OS X and UNIX instead of Windows. Another fault comes from our assumptions. The ratings online that were such a hefty component of our rankings were done by the average user rather than a programmer and so have different criteria for satisfaction. Also the Mac’s price was a hindrance, part of this stems from the fact that is has such a good display, which adds $200 to the price. These are our personal opinions and we hope that you choose the perfect companion for you that will support you in these coming years. But even if you make a poor choice at least this companion is not eternal.
